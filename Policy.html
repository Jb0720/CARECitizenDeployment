CARE Citizen Privacy Policy

CARE Citizen is an undergraduate project that aims to address the air pollution problem by providing an open-source hardware design for increased air pollution data and a web platform for data visualization. This Privacy Policy applies to the CARE Citizen website.

Consent

By using the CARE Citizen website, you acknowledge giving consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its conditions.

Information Collection

We collect and store the information you input on our website. Upon registration, we will ask for your name, address, email address, and your set password. For data collection, upon configuration of your node, we shall receive the following data parameters: the CO2, PM2.5, PM10, and the latitude-longitude location of your node.

We collect the said information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the individual data visualization service
  • To assist and support the users
  • To be able to contact the users

We collect the personal information you provide to us through web forms. We receive the node data upon setting up your node with the corresponding API.
